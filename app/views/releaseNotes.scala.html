@Messages("releaseNotes.title")

In this version you can:

  • set up a Government Gateway account
  • register your personal details
  • verify your identity
  • register your business details
  • go to the dashboard
  • link a property to your business
  • view your linked properties
  • view detailed assessments associated with those properties
  • start and complete a check against any of the assessments

When you start a check, you can choose to:

  • confirm or change the information held for this assessment
  • tell the VOA about external factors affecting the property
  • split an assessment into one or more assessments
  • merge an assessment with other assessments
  • remove an assessment
  • tell the VOA about a legal decision that affects an assessment
