@Messages("releaseNotes.title")

In this version you can:

  • set up a Government Gateway account
  • register your personal details
  • verify your identity
  • register your business details
  • go to the dashboard

You must then log out and wait 24 hours whilst we upload your details.

You can then log back in to:

  • view your linked properties in the dashboard
  • view detailed assessments associated with those properties
  • start and complete a check against any of the assessments

When you start a check, you can choose to:

  • tell the VOA about external factors affecting the property
  • split an assessment into one or more assessments
  • merge an assessment with other assessments
  • remove an assessment
  • tell the VOA about a legal decision that affects an assessment

Known issues

There are issues that we’re aware of and are currently working to fix:

  • the login screen has the wrong contact links
  • if you see ‘no record’ on the property features page within the check form, you’ll need to edit it and put in a value to progress with the form
  • when entering your address, you’ll need to use the postcode search tool rather than entering the address manually, in order to create your account correctly
